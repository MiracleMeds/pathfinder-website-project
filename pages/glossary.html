Glossary

This will provide a short explination as to what each tag used in the action list means.

  • Attack

    An ability with this trait involves an attack. For each attack you make beyond the first on your turn, you take a multiple attack penalty. (-5 to hit on the next strike made and -10 on the third. This bonus cannot exceed -10.)

  • Manipulate

    You must physically manipulate an item or make gestures to use an action with this trait. Creatures without a suitable appendage canâ€™t perform actions with this trait. Manipulate actions often trigger reactions.

  • Move

    An action with this trait involves moving from one space to another.
